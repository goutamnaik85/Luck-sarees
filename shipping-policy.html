Shipping Policy

Last updated: January 2026

1. Delivery Timelines

Orders are typically delivered within 3–7 business days for metro cities and 5–10 business days for other locations, after dispatch.

2. Shipping Charges

Free shipping on orders above ₹1,999. A flat shipping fee of ₹79 applies to orders below this amount.

3. Order Tracking

Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking link by email and SMS. You can also track orders anytime from the My Orders page.

4. Serviceable Locations

We currently ship to all serviceable PIN codes across India via our courier partners.

5. Delays

Delivery timelines may be affected during festive seasons, extreme weather, or courier disruptions. We'll notify you of any significant delays.
