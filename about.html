Our Story

Woven with Purpose

Lucky Collection began in the lanes of Chickpet, Bengaluru, where our founders grew up surrounded by silk merchants and the rhythmic clatter of handlooms. What started as a single family stall has grown into an online destination for saree lovers across India — without ever losing its connection to the weavers who make each piece possible.

Every saree we sell is sourced directly from weaver clusters in Karnataka and Tamil Nadu, cutting out layers of middlemen so that more of what you pay reaches the hands that wove it. We work closely with over 40 weaver families, offering fair, transparent pricing and steady demand for their craft.

Today, Lucky Collection ships pan-India — from Kanjivaram silk for weddings to breathable handloom cotton for everyday wear — while staying true to where we started: honest sourcing, fair prices, and sarees you can trust.
