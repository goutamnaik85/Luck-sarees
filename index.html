No — to keep pricing transparent and secure, we accept online payments only (UPI, cards, net banking, wallets) via Razorpay.

Most orders are delivered within 3–7 business days depending on your location. You'll get tracking details by email and SMS.
Can I return a saree?▾
Yes, unworn sarees with tags intact can be returned within 7 days of delivery. See our Refund Policy for details.

Yes — we source directly from weaver clusters across Karnataka and Tamil Nadu, and every listing states the exact fabric and weave.
